Past Projects

5000 masks for Dallas Independent School District

During the peak of the coronavirus pandemic, the Dallas Touch-A-Life team executed a project to donate 5000 masks for Dallas ISD. Hit extremely hard by the pandemic, most students in the district cannot afford to buy laptops or reliable internet, and could not be involved with the virtual school year. To accomodate for those who could not afford the demands of a virtual year, the team donated these masks to allow those attending in person school to learn in a safer environment.

100 hygiene kits for a local homeless shelter

A large amount of people during the coronavirus pandemic lacked essential personal protective equipment needed to maintain personal hygiene. The team took initiative by reaching out to homeless shelters needing more equipment. In each hygiene kit, we packed toothbrushes, shampoo, body wash, body spray, and many more necessities.

Clothes for Goodwill donation

Having adequate clothing is an essential part of our day to day lives. Unexpected weather changes are unavoidable, and without necessary clothing, we are left with potential detrimental health effects. Rather than throwing out their clothes, the team donated them to a local goodwill. Donating items like sweatshirts, shorts and pants, the team gave the underprivileged clothing for all types of weather.

Volunteering at the North Texas Food Bank

Food, despite seeming extremely common, can be a luxury for many. Texas has a food security percentage of 18.4%, which is higher than the national food security percentage by 7.9%. The North Texas Food Bank works to reduce that number, by donating fresh, packaged meals to the underprivileged across Texas. Our team volunteered at the food bank during a time of need by packaging food for delivery

About Touch-A-Life Texas

Touch-A-Life Texas is a branch of the main registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization Touch-A-Life foundation. As a branch, we work to help the underprivileged and help those in need in our society because we believe spreading kindness will positively impact our world. To spread word of our efforts, we utilize social media marketing and donation grants to fund our projects, and work toward the foundations goal for the betterment of society.
