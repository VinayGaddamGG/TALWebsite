About Us

What is Our Mission?

We develop creative and effective solutions to give back to the underprivileged in our community. By donating to our communities, we strive to spread positivity toward the movement of a kinder world.

Who are we?

We are not here merely to make a living. We are here to enrich the world." - Woodrow Wilson

We are the Touch-A-Life Texas team, a group of passionate, motivated members who look to make the world a better place on a daily basis

What do we do?

"We are what we do, especially what we do to change what we are." - Eduardo Galeano

We plan and execute donation projects which are intended to give back to the underprivileged in our community

Why do we donate?

"In anything we do, any endeavor, it's not what you do; it's why you do it." - Howard Schultz

We serve the underprivileged because we believe by donating, we can support their lives and allow them to live in their fullest potential.

Senior members

Meet the senior Touch-A-Life members, and their contribution to reducing poverty.

Vinay Gaddam Co-founder/Website Developer Vinay Gaddam is a current University of Texas at Dallas majoring in computer science, Co-founder of the Touch-A-Life Texas team, and creator of this website. Vinay joined the club during the peak of the coronavirus pandemic after realizing the effects of the pandemic on those in need. Living in an area where poverty is not as prevalent, the club opened his eyes to the problem of poverty in Texas, and around the world. Becoming inspired to battle against poverty, Vinay become heavily involved in the donation projects, leading procurement efforts for the donation of 5000 masks, 100 hygiene kits, and engaging in marketting efforts. Despite graduating high school, Vinay is actively looking to use his computer science skills to continue to help those in need, and in the future, he hopes to think of innovative, creative solutions to fuel the push for ending poverty.

About Touch-A-Life Texas

Touch-A-Life Texas is a branch of the main registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization Touch-A-Life foundation. As a branch, we work to help the underprivileged and help those in need in our society because we believe spreading kindness will positively impact our world. To spread word of our efforts, we utilize social media marketing and donation grants to fund our projects, and work toward the foundations goal for the betterment of society.
