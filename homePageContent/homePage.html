Poverty in Texas

"Overcoming poverty is not a gesture of charity. It is an act of justice" - Nelson Mandela

Given Texas's extremely large population of nearly 29 million people, many people face unideal living conditions on a daily basis. The information below are just a few alarming statistics of those living without the essential needs to live on a daily basis

More than 25,000 people are homeless

With the amount of homeless people raising, the capacity and amount of homeless shelters are remaining constant. The need for shelter continue to rise, and the number of 25,000 is only set to increase while the coronavirus pandemic continues to thrive in silence

Nearly 4 million people have food insecurity

Food, along with water and shelter, are essential things needed to live and sustain ones self on a daily basis. The lack of access to food, however, bring detrimental effects on ones health. Starvation can lead to malnutrition, which causes long term effects on the bones and body of a person. The number of those in food security is set to increase in the coming years, meaning more and more people will be left without food

3.98 million people in poverty

Despite the number of people in poverty decreasing over the past 6 years, the poverty rate continues to be one of the highest in the nation, and is above the nation average by 1.7%. Poverty in Texas will continue to thrive if we do not take initiative to stop the problem.

How are we making a change?

We organize and execute donation projects for those in need. By donating to the underprivileged, we hope to spread awareness of the poverty in Texas in an effort to collaborate with shelters, organizations, and other non-profits to join the push for ending poverty

About Touch-A-Life Texas

Touch-A-Life Texas is a branch of the main registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization Touch-A-Life foundation. As a branch, we work to help the underprivileged and help those in need in our society because we believe spreading kindness will positively impact our world. To spread word of our efforts, we utilize social media marketing and donation grants to fund our projects, and work toward the foundations goal for the betterment of society.
